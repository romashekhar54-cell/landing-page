Core Capabilities

Everything You Need to Manage
Documents at Enterprise Scale

KareDocs is engineered with features that eliminate document chaos — from intelligent indexing to end-to-end audit compliance.

01
Enterprise Search

With OCR-based full-text search, easily locate the documents you need in no time. Discover unparalleled speed and efficiency in locating documents across various contexts and departments.

02
Versatile File Management

KareDocs understands your diverse data needs, therefore we handle all file types - from images, documents, to media files, and zip files.

03
Role-Based Access Control

Define granular permissions at folder, document, or field level. Ensure only authorized users can view, edit, or share sensitive documents.

04
Workflow Automation

Automate document routing, approvals, and escalations. Build custom workflows without code slash TAT by up to 300% across departments.

05
Compliance & Audit Trails

Every action is logged who accessed, edited, or shared a document and when. Stay audit-ready for regulators, internal teams, and clients.

06
Version Control

Track every revision of every document. Restore any previous version instantly, compare changes, and maintain a complete edit history.

07
Analytics & Reporting

Real-time dashboards on document volumes, user activity, workflow status, and compliance metrics. Export reports for leadership review.

08
Intelligent Data Processing (IDP)

Leveraging AI innovations, KareDocs enables smooth and unified extraction and retrieval of data from both structured and unstructured data types.

09
On-Premise & Cloud Deployment

Deploy KareDocs on your own servers or in the cloud. Hybrid models supported. Full data sovereignty with enterprise-grade encryption.

Simple Process

How KareDocs Works

From document capture to intelligent retrieval the entire journey is automated, secure, and auditable.

01
Capture & Digitize

Bring all your documents into one system from physical files, emails, uploads, or existing applications. KareDocs ensures every document is captured accurately and ready for processing.

02
Organize & Structure

Documents are automatically organized using intelligent classification and metadata tagging, making them easy to manage without manual effort.

03
Automate & Process

Streamline document movement with automated workflows for approvals, validations, and processing reducing delays and manual follow-ups.

04
Store Securely

Approved documents are stored in a highly secure environment with controlled access, version history, and complete data protection whether on premise, cloud, or hybrid. your data stays where you need it.

05
Retrieve in Seconds

Any document can be found in under 3 seconds using full-text search, metadata filters, or saved search queries. Share securely with internal or external stakeholders via permissions-controlled links.
